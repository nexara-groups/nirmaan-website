Our Practice

Design that serves people first

Sai Nirmaan Architects is a full-spectrum design practice led by Principal Architect Suresh Kumar Bandaru. Over 15 years, we have delivered Architectural Design and Landscape Design projects across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and Odisha — from intimate family residences to large-scale institutional commissions. 300+ Projects. 4+ States.

We believe a building's true measure is not how it photographs, but how it performs for the people who use it every day. Every project begins with listening.

Featured Project

Tribal Museum —
President's Village

A cultural landmark that celebrates the heritage and identity of tribal communities through architecture. A series of interlocking pavilions, native landscaping, and material honesty create an experience that is both civic and deeply human.

Realised through detailed Enscape visualisation, this project marks a significant new direction for the practice in cultural and civic architecture.

Principal Architect

Suresh Kumar Bandaru

With a B.Arch from SAR College of Architecture (Affiliated to JNAFAU), Suresh has spent 15+ years shaping the built environment across South India and Odisha. His practice encompasses everything from luxury residences to disaster-resilient housing built alongside UNDP, UNICEF and Save the Children.

What distinguishes the practice is its belief that architecture must serve people first — that the measure of a building is the quality of life it enables, not the awards it collects.

Social Impact

Architecture that serves
the most vulnerable

One of the practice's defining chapters is its partnership with international humanitarian organisations on the Disaster Resilient & Enclosed Shelter (DRES) programme — building climate-resilient, dignified housing for cyclone-affected communities along the Andhra Pradesh coastline.

This work reshaped how we think about design. When your client has nothing, every decision matters more — not less.
