01
Residential Spaces

Homes Designed Around How You Live

Every home we design begins with a conversation about life — how your family moves, gathers, rests, and grows. We translate this understanding into architecture that is beautiful, functional, and uniquely yours.

We work with individual homeowners, developers, and housing societies across Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, Vijayawada, and Odisha — from single villas to multi-unit residential complexes.

  • Site analysis and feasibility assessment
  • Concept design and schematic plans
  • Detailed architectural drawings and specifications
  • Interior design integration
  • Structural coordination and services integration
  • Liaison with local planning authorities
  • Construction supervision and quality oversight
02
Commercial Spaces

Buildings That Work Hard — and Look the Part

Commercial buildings are a brand in physical form. Whether it's a corporate office, a healthcare facility, or a retail environment, we design spaces that communicate your organisation's identity while maximising operational performance.

We have experience working with corporate clients including India Bulls Housing Finance, Clove Dental, and institutional bodies across the region.

  • Commercial office and co-working spaces
  • Healthcare and clinical environments
  • Retail and hospitality design
  • Educational and institutional buildings
  • Industrial and mixed-use developments
  • Brand-aligned architectural identity
03
Interior Design

Interiors That Elevate Everyday Life

We approach Interior Design as a natural extension of Architecture — not an afterthought. Every material, every light source, every detail of joinery contributes to a coherent spatial experience that feels intentional from the moment you walk in.

Our Interior Design portfolio spans luxury residential apartments, dental clinics, restaurants, and corporate offices across Hyderabad, Visakhapatnam, and Andhra Pradesh.

  • Residential interior design (apartments, villas, penthouses)
  • Commercial interiors (offices, clinics, restaurants)
  • Custom furniture and joinery design
  • Lighting design and specification
  • Material and finish selection
  • 3D visualisation and render packages
  • Coordination with contractors and vendors
04
Landscape Design

Outdoor Spaces That Complete the Picture

Great buildings deserve great surroundings. Our Landscape Design practice creates outdoor environments — from intimate private gardens to large public spaces — that complement the built form and enhance the lives of those who use them.

We have designed Landscape schemes for government institutions (GVMC, Police Department), private developers (Swathi Promoters), and healthcare campuses (KIMS Hospitals).

  • Residential garden and courtyard design
  • Campus and institutional landscape planning
  • Public realm and streetscape design
  • Planting design with native species
  • Water features and hardscape design
  • Irrigation and maintenance planning

06
Project Management

Your Project, Delivered as Designed

Good design is only as good as its execution. We offer comprehensive project management and construction supervision — ensuring the quality, intent, and detail of the design are faithfully realised on site.

For clients who require it, we act as the single point of accountability across all disciplines — managing contractors, consultants, and authorities so you don't have to.

  • Construction documentation and specifications
  • Tender management and contractor selection
  • Site supervision and quality control
  • Milestone-based project tracking
  • Budget and cost management
  • Authority liaison and approvals
  • Handover and post-completion support
How We Work

Our Design Process

A structured approach that keeps you informed, in control, and excited at every stage.

1

Discover

We listen first — understanding your brief, your site, your budget, and your vision before a single line is drawn.

2

Concept

We develop design concepts and present them for your feedback — exploring options, testing ideas, and refining direction.

3

Design

Approved concepts are developed into detailed architectural drawings, 3D renders, and full specifications.

4

Deliver

We oversee construction, coordinate with contractors, and ensure the built result matches the design intent.

5

Handover

We walk you through the completed project, address any snagging, and ensure you're fully satisfied before handover.
